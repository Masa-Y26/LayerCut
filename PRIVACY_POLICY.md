# Privacy Policy for LayerCut

**Effective Date:** April 2026

## Overview

LayerCut ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

## Information Collection

### Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do NOT collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number
- We do NOT collect location data
- We do NOT collect device identifiers or user tracking information
- We do NOT store or transmit images you process in the App

### Local Processing

- All image processing and editing performed in LayerCut is done locally on your device
- Images are not uploaded to our servers or any third-party services
- Your image files remain entirely under your control

### Advertising

- The App may display advertisements to support free usage
- Third-party ad networks may collect limited information to serve relevant ads (such as general app usage patterns)
- You may see advertisements based on your general interests, not personal data we collect

## Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services for:
- Analytics (to understand app performance and usage patterns)
- Advertising delivery
- Crash reporting and performance monitoring

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us immediately.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access and review what information we hold about you
- Request deletion of any personal information
- Opt out of analytics and advertising tracking where possible

You can manage ad preferences and tracking settings through your iOS device settings.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the effective date at the top of this policy.
